Government Schemes

🌱

PM-KISAN

Direct income support of ₹6,000 per year to eligible farmer families in three equal installments.

Eligibility:

  • ✓ Small and marginal farmers
  • ✓ Land ownership documentation
  • ✓ Aadhaar card linked to bank account
🌊

PMKSY

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana provides support for micro-irrigation and water conservation.

Benefits:

  • ✓ Up to 55% subsidy on micro-irrigation
  • ✓ Technical support for implementation
  • ✓ Water conservation infrastructure
🌿

PKVY

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana promotes organic farming through cluster approach and certification.

Support Provided:

  • ✓ ₹50,000 per hectare for 3 years
  • ✓ Certification and marketing support
  • ✓ Training and capacity building
